Chicken Alfredo Pasta

BY ALBERT COOK | JUNE 2016

TRENDING:

An Italian sauce that is usually combined with fettuccine noodles and is referred to as fettuccine Alfredo. The sauce consists of heavy cream or half and half that is mixed with butter, grated Parmesan cheese, pepper, and occasionally nutmeg to create a rich Italian meal.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED:

  • 1. 8 ounces dried fettuccine pasta
  • 2. 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3. 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 4. 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black
    pepper, divided
  • 5. 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 6. 8 tablespoons (1 stick) European-style
    unsalted butter, divided 2 cloves garlic.
  • 7. 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8. 1 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 9. 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 10. Coarsely chopped fresh parsley leaves

Video receipe of Chicken Alfredo Pasta:

PROCESS:

1.Cook the pasta, drain, and reserve cooking water.

2.Dry the chicken and season with salt and pepper.

3.Dry the chicken and season with salt and pepper.

4.Add the chicken to the hot pan, and cook for 5 to
7 minutes.

5.Flip the chicken and cook for another 5 to 7 minutes,
until the chicken reaches 165°F.Melt the rest of
the butter and sauté the garlic.

6.Make the Alfredo sauce.Add the drained pasta and
toss to coat in the sauce.
