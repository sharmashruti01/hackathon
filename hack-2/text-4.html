INGREDIENTS REQUIRED:

  • 1. 1/4 c. Dutch process cocoa powder
  • 2. 5 oz. semisweet chocolate
  • 3. 1/4 c. neutral oil
  • 4. 1/2 c. sugar
  • 5. 1/2 c. packed dark brown sugar
  • 6. 1 large egg,2 large egg yolks
  • 7. 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 8.3/4 c. flour
  • 9. 2 tbsp. malted milk powder, optional
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 6 oz. milk chocolate, coarsely chopped

Video receipe of Malted Forever Brownies:

PROCESS:

1.In a large heatproof bowl, whisk the cocoa powder
and 1/4 c.boiling water until smooth.

2.Add the semisweet chocolate, butter and oil to the bowl
with the cocoa mixture and set it over a
medium saucepan filled with about 1 inch of simmering

3. Warm the mixture gently, stirring occasionally, until the
chocolate and butter are melted

4.Add the flour, malted milk powder and salt and whisk
slowly until everything is combined

5.Add the milk chocolate to the batter and fold with a
flexible spatula to distribute.

6.Bake the brownies until the surface is shiny and puffed and
the center is dry to the touch but still soft when pressed.
